How ordering & payment works

No app, no card. Build your order, pay our Paybill, and our staff confirms it on the spot.

01

Build your order

Add dishes from the menu above to your cart.

02

Get your reference

Checkout gives you a Paybill number and a unique order reference.

03

Pay via M-Pesa

Lipa na M-Pesa → Pay Bill → enter the number and your reference as the account number.

04

We confirm it

Tap "I've paid" — our staff checks the till and confirms your order.

This is a working prototype using manual confirmation by staff. A future version of Njiwa's system can confirm payment automatically the moment it lands, once Njiwa is set up directly with Safaricom for that.
